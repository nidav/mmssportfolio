About MMSS

The Monash Marketing Student Society (MMSS) is one of the elusive student societies at Monash University. We offer a variety of events ranging from parties and balls to corporate networking events and masterclasses. We are committed to ensuring our members enjoy a smooth transition from high school to university, and ultimately into an rewarding career.
